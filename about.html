Our story

From a spare room to a working craft.

Acoustic started the way most careful things start — as a problem we had ourselves. A home studio that sounded like a hallway. Off-the-shelf panels that looked like they belonged in a call centre.

So we built our own. Pine frames from a local mill. Dense mineral wool layered to hit the acoustic numbers that matter. Fabric stretched by hand. Then we started printing artwork on them.

What began as a weekend hobby turned into a waiting list. We still build every panel ourselves — measured, cut, wrapped, and inspected by a human. That's slow, and we like it that way.

If you're here, you probably care about your room more than most people do. We'd love to help you make it sound as good as it looks.
